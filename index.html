About RecipePal

RecipePal is a native Android application developed with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose. Users can choose a dietary preference, search recipes, save favorites, and view ingredients and cooking instructions.

Features

🥗 Diet Filtering

Browse vegetarian, vegan, pescetarian, kosher, heritage, and other recipe categories.

🔍 Recipe Search

Search by recipe name, country, dietary category, description, or ingredient.

❤️ Favorites

Save favorite recipes and access them from the Favorites screen.

📖 Recipe Details

View ingredients, instructions, preparation time, cooking time, servings, and allergens.

Built With

Kotlin Jetpack Compose Material 3 MVVM StateFlow Android Studio
