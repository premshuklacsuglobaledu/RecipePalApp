About RecipePal

RecipePal is a native Android recipe recommendation app developed with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose. Users can select dietary preferences, search recipes, save favorites, and view ingredients and cooking instructions.

Features

🥗 Diet Filtering

Find recipes based on vegetarian, vegan, pescetarian, kosher, heritage, and other preferences.

🔍 Recipe Search

Search recipes by name, country, dietary category, or ingredient.

❤️ Favorites

Save preferred recipes and quickly access them from the Favorites screen.

📖 Recipe Details

View ingredients, cooking steps, preparation time, servings, calories, and allergen information.
