About RecipePal

RecipePal helps users discover recipes from around the world. Browse recipes based on vegetarian, vegan, pescetarian, heritage, and many other dietary preferences.

Features

🥗 Diet Preferences

Select from multiple dietary lifestyles.

🔍 Recipe Search

Quickly search for recipes and ingredients.

❤️ Favorites

Save recipes you enjoy for later.

📖 Recipe Details

Ingredients, instructions, calories, prep time, allergens, and more.
